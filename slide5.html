My Minor

Digital Humanities

An interdisciplinary field that integrates digital technology into research and teaching within the humanities — using computational methods to analyze and interpret humanities-based research.

The Problem

The Digital Divide

The gap between those who have access to technology and those who don't.

But it's not just about access — it's about literacy, skills, and meaningful use.

Everyone's racing to advance AI.

Even here at Creighton.

But we're leaving people behind.

The more we advance,
the wider the divide grows.

The Initiative

Digital Literacy

An initiative to bridge the gap — to make sure no one gets left behind as technology moves forward.
