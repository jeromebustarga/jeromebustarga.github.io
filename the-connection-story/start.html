The Connection

An Interactive Story About the Internet Age

The year is 2010. You're 14 years old, and your parents just got Wi-Fi installed at home. For the first time, a world of infinite possibilities glows from the screen in front of you.

Your older cousin showed you the basics: email, search engines, websites. But now you're alone with it. The cursor blinks, waiting. The whole world is suddenly accessible from your bedroom.

"Welcome to the Internet. Where would you like to go today?"

Your heart races a little. This feels big. Like standing at the edge of something that will change everything.

What draws you in first?

Path A People are talking about this thing called "social media" — a place where you can connect with anyone, anywhere. You want to see what all the hype is about. Path B You've always been curious about how things work. Someone told you that you can learn literally anything online — for free. That sounds incredible.
