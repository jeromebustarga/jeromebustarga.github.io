The Performance

It works. Your carefully curated posts get hundreds of likes. You angle your photos just right. You only share the highlights. People think you're living an amazing life.

But there's a cost. You spend hours crafting the perfect caption. A post that doesn't perform well ruins your evening. You refresh obsessively, watching the numbers.

"Only 34 likes? Sarah got 200 on hers. What did I do wrong?"

You start comparing yourself constantly. Their vacations. Their relationships. Their success. The gap between your curated life and your real life grows wider every day.

One night, at 2 AM, you can't sleep. You've spent six hours scrolling, and you feel worse than when you started. The phone glows in the dark. You wonder if this is what life is now.

What happens next?
