✦ Across the Ocean

It's 2025 now. You're 29 years old.

Your grandmother passed away last year. But you have hundreds of hours of video calls saved. Her stories about your grandfather. Her recipes, demonstrated step by step. Her face lighting up every time she saw yours on the screen.

The internet gave you fifteen extra years with her that distance would have stolen. She met your partner through a screen. She watched you graduate, crying happily from 8,000 miles away.

"Technology didn't replace human connection. It extended it across oceans and time zones."

You still watch those old recordings sometimes. Her laugh. Her advice. Her love, preserved in pixels and data, as real as the day it was recorded.

The internet, at its best, is a bridge. You used it to connect to what mattered most.
