Key Questions to Answer

I organized my brainstorm into key questions that would guide the design and technical decisions.

üë•

Who are the primary users?

Current iJay students looking for career inspiration and mentorship opportunities. Prospective students evaluating the program. Faculty showcasing program outcomes.

üéØ

What do users need to find?

Alumni in their field of interest, people at companies they want to work for, graduates from their same year, or those with specific skills they're developing.

‚öôÔ∏è

How will this be maintained?

Future student teams will need to add new alumni without developer knowledge. The system must be self-documenting and use Shopify-native tools.

üìà

How will this scale?

Starting with 10-15 alumni but potentially growing to 100+. The interface must remain usable and performant at scale.

üîó

What data do we need?

Name, photo, graduation year, practicum cohort, team assignment, current title, company, skills, bio, and personal testimonial quote.

üì±

How will this work on mobile?

Many students browse on phones. The two-panel layout must collapse to a single column list on mobile while maintaining usability.
