Computer Science Graphic Design Digital Humanities Creighton University

Filipino Heritage

Born and raised in the Philippines, I bring a unique cultural perspective to my work. This background deeply influences my commitment to inclusive design—creating experiences that work for communities facing digital divides, language barriers, and limited connectivity.

Cultural Design Inclusive UX Digital Accessibility Community Impact

I believe Filipino users deserve design that reflects their visual culture—bold, culturally-inspired palettes rather than generic corporate aesthetics. Every project is an opportunity to craft something that resonates authentically with its audience.

Brand Identity Visual Storytelling Cultural Authenticity User Advocacy

Building sophisticated web applications with modern frameworks and clean architecture. From Django and Spring Boot backends to interactive p5.js experiences, I love turning complex ideas into elegant solutions that perform beautifully across all devices.

Django & Python JavaScript & p5.js Spring Boot Responsive Design

HDR Inc. BrookHaven IWS Team Collaboration Client Relations

The Algorithm Mirror

My research project "The Algorithm Mirror" visualizes YouTube recommendation algorithms through aesthetic data art, making complex algorithmic systems tangible and understandable. This work reflects my commitment to transparency and privacy-first design.

Data Visualization Algorithm Transparency Privacy-First Design Academic Research
