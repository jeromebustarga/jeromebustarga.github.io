The Digital Divide in the Philippines

Connection is a privilege,
not a guarantee.

In a nation of 7,641 islands, internet access isn't just about speed — it's about opportunity, education, and survival.

The Reality

Geography shouldn't determine your future.

Palawan

Students climb mountains for signal

In remote barangays, students trek hours to hilltops just to submit assignments. One missed deadline can mean a failing grade — through no fault of their own.

Mindanao

Farmers cut off from markets

Without reliable internet, farmers can't access real-time market prices. Middlemen exploit this gap, buying crops for a fraction of their worth.

Visayas

Telemedicine remains a dream

Island communities with no doctors hoped telemedicine would help. But without stable connections, virtual consultations are impossible.
