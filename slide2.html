01 — The Origin

Let's go back to the beginning.

Philippines • 2011

I grew up in the Philippines.

In 2011, our family got our very first computer.

For the first time,
I had access to a computer.
To the internet.

To a world I'd only heard
my classmates talk about.

I played Plants vs. Zombies.
I made my first Facebook account.
I discovered Pizap —
a photo editing tool.

My Very First Designs

And this is what I made.

2014

Then the typhoon hit.

We almost lost our house.
A portion of our roof stayed strong —
but the area where we kept the computer
wasn't so lucky.

It got wet. It broke.

2019 • Gap Year

I took a gap year.

Then 2020 hit — the pandemic.
My dad lost his job.

We needed to do something.
With my dad being a baker,
we started a pizza shop and bakery.

We needed a social media presence.

So I volunteered.

The only thing I had was
my Android phone.

Promotional Posters • Made on Phone
2020 • Pandemic

When things started running again,
I went back to school — online.

I spent my last two years of high school
on my phone.

Balancing business and school
wasn't easy.

But persistence paid off.

I graduated top of my class.

And got offered a full ride
to study in the US.

Creighton University

I started as a Physics major
on the pre-engineering track.

But thanks to the tight-knit community,
I met a lot of friends.

Including Sarah —
a graphic design graduate
who introduced me to CSDJ.

I was so interested in the work she was doing.

And that's where my design journey
at Creighton began.

I was taught. Nurtured. Guided.
By amazing professors —
in both graphic design and computer science.
