The Journey of Refinement

This brand identity underwent a significant evolution over two years. The initial designs in 2023 explored various approaches and logo types, establishing the foundational concept of merging aerospace innovation with environmental responsibility. After reflection and growth, the brand was revisited in 2025 with refined execution, improved color harmony, and a more cohesive visual system.

Refined Brand Identity (2025)

The refined logo masterfully integrates a rocket motif with green leaves forming a circular embrace, symbolizing commitment to environmental sustainability. Brand Story

The meaning behind the name and vision

The Name

The name "GreenX Aerospace" embodies a powerful fusion of environmental stewardship and space exploration ambition. The "Green" represents our unwavering commitment to sustainable practices in aerospace, while the "X" symbolizes the endless possibilities and unknowns we seek to discover in the vast cosmos, reflecting our dedication to pushing boundaries while preserving our planet.

The Tagline

"Sustainable skies, infinite horizons" captures our dual mission in a concise yet profound way. It emphasizes our innovative approach to space exploration that prioritizes environmental responsibility ("sustainable skies") while highlighting our boundless aspirations for discovery and advancement ("infinite horizons"), creating a perfect balance between progress and preservation.

Refinement Process

Technical execution from sketch to final design

Step 01

Digital Sketching

Using Sketchbook app to explore compositions, establishing basic forms and proportions.

Step 02

Line Refinement

Using pen tool to create cleaner, more defined lines and perfect visual relationships.

Step 03

Vector & Color

Implementation in Adobe Illustrator with sophisticated gradient palette and precise execution.

My design process began with digital sketching using the Sketchbook app, where I explored various compositions combining the rocket and leaf elements. After finalizing the sketch, I moved to Adobe Illustrator for vector implementation. I carefully traced and refined the outlines, ensuring smooth curves and precise geometric shapes. The color application was crucial—I created a sophisticated gradient palette transitioning from light green to deep navy blue, symbolizing the connection between Earth and space. The typography for "GREENX AEROSPACE" and the tagline was meticulously set, perfecting spacing, alignment, and visual hierarchy. The final result is a harmonious blend of organic and technological elements, unified by thoughtful color strategy and precise execution.
